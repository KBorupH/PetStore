Who We Are

Founded in 2010, Paws & Claws Pet Store began as a small family-owned shop with a simple mission: to provide the highest quality products and services for pets and their families. Over the years, we've grown into a trusted community destination where pet owners can find everything they need for their beloved companions.

Our commitment to animal welfare extends beyond our store walls. We actively partner with local animal shelters and rescue organizations, hosting adoption events and providing support to pets in need. We believe that every pet deserves a loving home and the best care possible.
